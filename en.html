How it works

Start Improving in 3 Steps

No complex setup required. Just open the app, start, and let the advanced AI watch over your habits.

Step 01

Select Activity & Start

Choose what you are doing—studying, working, or reading—and start. Just prop up your iPhone and face the camera.

Step 02

Real-time AI Monitoring

The TrueDepth camera analyzes subtle facial muscles. If your mouth stays open for over 3 seconds, a gentle vibration notifies you without distractions.

Step 03

Check Session Analysis

Detection counts, unconscious duration, and improvement scores are automatically tallied. The AI objectively analyzes your habit trends for each activity.

Features

習慣を変える、本格的なFeatures

単なるタイマーではありません。無意識の習慣を自覚し、改善へと導くために設計されたプレミアムなFeatures群です。

Real-time Detection

High-precision face recognition using Apple TrueDepth API & ARKit. Our unique EMA smoothing eliminates temporary noise to capture accurately only when the habit truly occurs.

Smart Notification System

Get notified with haptic feedback (vibration) the moment a habit is detected. Flexibly switch between a silent mode for quiet places like cafes and an audio notification.

Advanced Session Analysis

Visualize not only detection count but also open-mouth duration and average awareness speed with beautiful graphs. AI automatically reports trends, such as "frequently occurs while reading."

Completely Offline & Secure

すべてのAI画像処理はあなたのiPhoneデバイス内で完結します。顔の映像データが外部サーバーに送信されることは物理的にあり得ません。Privacyを最優先しています。

Automated Snapshot Logging

Automatically capture a face snapshot the moment a habit is detected (can be toggled on/off). Looking back at your objective state dramatically increases awareness for improvement.

Continuous Streak Tracking

ゲーム感覚で毎日の記録を習慣化。連続日数を計測し、バッジでお祝いすることで、モチベーションを維持しながら長期的なクセとの決別をSupportします。

ポリシーとSupport

All information processing is completed within your iPhone device. Camera video or depth data for face recognition is never sent to external servers, neither intentionally nor systematically.

1. Use of TrueDepth API (Face Recognition Data)

We use the TrueDepth API (ARKit) provided by Apple to obtain highly accurate facial expression data.

  • 利用目的：セッション実行中の口の開閉状態をリアルタイムで判別し、無意識のクセを検知した際にバイブレーション等のフィードバックを返すという、アプリのコアFeaturesの提供目的のみに使用します。
  • Data Handling: The acquired face depth data and camera video stream are used strictly in memory for real-time calculation at that moment. They are never persisted (saved), sent to external servers via the internet, or shared with third parties.

2. Local Storage of Recorded Data

All data voluntarily recorded by the user within the app is saved exclusively to the local storage of the user’s own iPhone device. If the user uninstalls this app, this data is completely deleted from the device by the OS mechanisms.

3. No Use of Third-Party Tools

This app incorporates absolutely no external analytics tools (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, various advertising SDKs, etc.) that would collect user behavior history, behavioral statistics, crash logs, etc. without permission.

4. Policy Revisions & Notifications

法令・ガイドラインの変更、または新Featuresの追加に伴い、必要に応じて本Privacyポリシーを改定することがあります。重要な変更があった場合は、App Store のアップデート内容（リリースノート）やアプリ内のお知らせ等で明確に明示します。
